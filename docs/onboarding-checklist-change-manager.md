# Change Manager Onboarding Checklist

Welcome to OctoAcme! This checklist will help you get started in your Change Manager role.

## Week 1: Orientation and Context

### Understanding OctoAcme
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) documentation
- [ ] Understand company values and change management philosophy
- [ ] Meet with your manager to discuss role expectations and success metrics

### Team Introductions
- [ ] Meet with Project Managers you'll be working with
- [ ] Introduction meeting with Stakeholder Liaison team
- [ ] Connect with Product Managers to understand product roadmap
- [ ] Meet key stakeholders across business units

### Tools and Access
- [ ] Access to project management tools (boards, tracking systems)
- [ ] Access to communication platforms (email, chat, video conferencing)
- [ ] Access to documentation repositories and knowledge bases
- [ ] Review templates for change impact assessments and communication plans

## Week 2: Process Deep Dive

### Change Management Framework
- [ ] Review current change initiatives and their status
- [ ] Study past change impact assessments and lessons learned
- [ ] Understand escalation procedures for change-related issues
- [ ] Review change communication templates and guidelines

### Stakeholder Mapping
- [ ] Identify key stakeholder groups for upcoming changes
- [ ] Create initial stakeholder impact and influence matrix
- [ ] Document stakeholder communication preferences and needs
- [ ] Establish regular touchpoint schedule with Stakeholder Liaison

### Project Lifecycle Integration
- [ ] Shadow a project from initiation through planning
- [ ] Observe handoff points between roles
- [ ] Understand quality gates and approval processes
- [ ] Review [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md) documentation

## Week 3-4: Active Engagement

### First Change Initiative
- [ ] Partner with Project Manager on an active project
- [ ] Conduct change impact assessment for upcoming release
- [ ] Draft communication plan for stakeholder groups
- [ ] Facilitate change readiness workshop with affected teams

### Building Relationships
- [ ] Schedule regular 1:1s with key Project Managers
- [ ] Establish working rhythm with Stakeholder Liaison
- [ ] Connect with Agile Coach on team readiness practices
- [ ] Build rapport with team members who will be change champions

### Deliverables and Metrics
- [ ] Complete your first change impact assessment
- [ ] Deliver first stakeholder readiness report
- [ ] Set up change adoption metrics tracking
- [ ] Present change management approach to leadership team

## Month 2+: Ongoing Development

### Continuous Improvement
- [ ] Gather feedback on change management approach
- [ ] Identify opportunities to improve change processes
- [ ] Participate in retrospectives and incorporate learnings
- [ ] Share best practices with other Change Managers

### Scaling Your Impact
- [ ] Document reusable change management templates
- [ ] Mentor team members on change management basics
- [ ] Contribute to organizational change capability building
- [ ] Identify and address systemic change adoption barriers

## Success Indicators (First 90 Days)

- [ ] Successfully supported at least 2 change initiatives
- [ ] Established trusted relationships with Project Managers and Stakeholder Liaisons
- [ ] Demonstrated value through improved stakeholder readiness
- [ ] Contributed to process improvements based on change insights
- [ ] Built foundation for scaling change management capabilities
