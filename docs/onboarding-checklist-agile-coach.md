# Agile Coach Onboarding Checklist

Welcome to OctoAcme! This checklist will help you get started in your Agile Coach role.

## Week 1: Orientation and Context

### Understanding OctoAcme
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) documentation
- [ ] Understand OctoAcme's agile maturity and current practices
- [ ] Meet with your manager to discuss coaching philosophy and approach

### Team Landscape
- [ ] Learn about team structures and current agile practices
- [ ] Identify teams at different stages of agile adoption
- [ ] Meet with Project Managers to understand delivery challenges
- [ ] Connect with Developers and understand technical practices

### Tools and Access
- [ ] Access to project boards and sprint management tools
- [ ] Access to team collaboration spaces and documentation
- [ ] Review retrospective history and team health metrics
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with coaching resources and templates

## Week 2: Assessment and Observation

### Team Observations
- [ ] Observe 3-5 team standups across different teams
- [ ] Attend sprint planning sessions to understand planning practices
- [ ] Shadow retrospectives to see current improvement patterns
- [ ] Review team velocity, burndown, and health metrics

### Coaching Opportunities
- [ ] Identify quick wins for team effectiveness
- [ ] Document impediments and organizational blockers
- [ ] Assess team dynamics and collaboration patterns
- [ ] Understand current pain points from team members' perspectives

### Stakeholder Alignment
- [ ] Meet with Product Managers to understand product priorities
- [ ] Connect with QA Leads on quality practices and testing approaches
- [ ] Align with Project Managers on delivery expectations
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) processes

## Week 3-4: Active Coaching

### First Coaching Engagement
- [ ] Partner with a team for hands-on coaching
- [ ] Facilitate your first retrospective at OctoAcme
- [ ] Coach Project Manager or Scrum Master on facilitation techniques
- [ ] Introduce or refine agile ceremony formats with a team

### Building Trust
- [ ] Establish 1:1 coaching relationships with team members
- [ ] Create safe space for teams to share challenges
- [ ] Demonstrate value through visible improvements
- [ ] Share relevant agile practices and case studies

### Process Improvements
- [ ] Help a team improve their standup effectiveness
- [ ] Support backlog refinement and story decomposition
- [ ] Introduce team health check or improvement metrics
- [ ] Coach on conflict resolution or team dysfunction

## Month 2+: Scaling Impact

### Community Building
- [ ] Establish agile community of practice or guild
- [ ] Create forums for cross-team learning and sharing
- [ ] Document and share successful practices and patterns
- [ ] Mentor other coaches or team facilitators

### Organizational Impediments
- [ ] Identify and work to remove systemic blockers
- [ ] Coach leadership on agile principles and mindset
- [ ] Advocate for team autonomy and empowerment
- [ ] Support organizational change toward agile culture

### Continuous Learning
- [ ] Gather feedback on coaching effectiveness
- [ ] Participate in coach supervision or peer learning
- [ ] Stay current with agile practices and industry trends
- [ ] Experiment with new coaching techniques and frameworks

## Success Indicators (First 90 Days)

- [ ] Coached at least 2-3 teams with measurable improvements
- [ ] Facilitated effective retrospectives with actionable outcomes
- [ ] Built trust and rapport with teams and leadership
- [ ] Identified and helped remove key organizational impediments
- [ ] Established foundation for agile community and continuous learning
