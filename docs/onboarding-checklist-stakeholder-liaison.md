# Stakeholder Liaison Onboarding Checklist

Welcome to OctoAcme! This checklist will help you get started in your Stakeholder Liaison role.

## Week 1: Orientation and Context

### Understanding OctoAcme
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) documentation
- [ ] Understand company structure and business units
- [ ] Meet with your manager to discuss role scope and success criteria

### Team Introductions
- [ ] Meet with Project Managers for active projects
- [ ] Connect with Product Managers to understand product strategy
- [ ] Introduction to Change Manager and alignment on communications
- [ ] Meet internal team members who interface with stakeholders

### Tools and Access
- [ ] Access to stakeholder management and CRM systems
- [ ] Access to communication platforms and distribution lists
- [ ] Review stakeholder database and contact information
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with stakeholder communication templates

## Week 2: Stakeholder Mapping

### Understanding Stakeholders
- [ ] Review existing stakeholder registers and profiles
- [ ] Study stakeholder engagement history and feedback
- [ ] Identify stakeholder groups, interests, and influence levels
- [ ] Understand decision-making processes and approval chains

### Communication Patterns
- [ ] Review past stakeholder communications and updates
- [ ] Understand communication cadences and preferences
- [ ] Learn escalation procedures for stakeholder issues
- [ ] Study [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md) guide

### Project Context
- [ ] Learn about current and upcoming projects
- [ ] Understand stakeholder impact from recent initiatives
- [ ] Review stakeholder feedback and satisfaction metrics
- [ ] Identify projects with high stakeholder engagement needs

## Week 3-4: Active Engagement

### First Stakeholder Interactions
- [ ] Shadow experienced Stakeholder Liaison in meetings
- [ ] Join stakeholder review sessions for active projects
- [ ] Make introductions to key stakeholders
- [ ] Send your first stakeholder update communication

### Building Relationships
- [ ] Schedule introductory 1:1s with major stakeholders
- [ ] Establish regular touchpoint schedule with Project Managers
- [ ] Create communication plan for assigned stakeholder groups
- [ ] Build rapport with internal champions and advocates

### Feedback Management
- [ ] Set up stakeholder feedback collection mechanisms
- [ ] Create process for logging and routing stakeholder requests
- [ ] Establish criteria for prioritizing stakeholder input
- [ ] Document stakeholder concerns and resolution tracking

## Month 2+: Ongoing Development

### Stakeholder Engagement
- [ ] Lead stakeholder review sessions independently
- [ ] Conduct stakeholder satisfaction surveys
- [ ] Facilitate requirements gathering workshops
- [ ] Manage escalations and resolve stakeholder concerns

### Process Optimization
- [ ] Refine stakeholder communication templates
- [ ] Improve feedback collection and routing processes
- [ ] Develop stakeholder personas or segmentation
- [ ] Create reusable engagement playbooks

### Strategic Relationships
- [ ] Deepen relationships with key influencers
- [ ] Identify and cultivate stakeholder champions
- [ ] Build trust through consistent, transparent communication
- [ ] Anticipate and proactively address stakeholder concerns

## Success Indicators (First 90 Days)

- [ ] Successfully managed stakeholder engagement for 2+ projects
- [ ] Established trusted relationships with key stakeholders
- [ ] Improved stakeholder satisfaction scores or feedback quality
- [ ] Reduced ad-hoc interruptions to project teams from stakeholders
- [ ] Documented effective stakeholder engagement patterns
